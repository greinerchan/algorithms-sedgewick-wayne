1.4.1

The total execution times for if statement is the product of execution times for all loops.

           freq
loop1       N
loop2     Σ(start at 1 end at n) （n - i)  = (n^2 - n)/2 
loop3    Σ(start at 0 end at n - 1)
        * Σ(start at j = i + 1 end at n - 1) 
        * Σ(start at k = j + 1 end at n - 1)
				= n^3 / 6 - n^2/2 + n/3
